## Our Commitment

**aadi_revobots_ai** is an open-source project maintained by [Revobots](https://revobots.ai) and used to build real-world robotics products for customers across industries. The quality of this codebase directly affects the reliability and safety of deployed robotic systems.

We are committed to maintaining a professional, inclusive, and respectful community — one where contributors, customers, partners, and maintainers can collaborate effectively and with confidence.

Everyone who contributes to, uses, or engages with this project is expected to uphold the standards set out in this document.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Expected Behaviour

- Communicating clearly, professionally, and respectfully at all times
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback graciously
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and working to correct them
- Prioritising the safety, reliability, and quality of code that may be deployed on physical robotic systems
- Respecting differing viewpoints, experiences, and technical approaches
- Being mindful that contributions may affect downstream customer products — thinking before merging
- Disclosing conflicts of interest transparently

### ❌ Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind — toward contributors, customers, or partners
- Submitting code known to be unsafe, unstable, or deliberately harmful for robotic deployments
- Sharing confidential customer information, proprietary datasets, or trade secrets without authorisation
- Misrepresenting your identity, affiliation, or the origin of your contributions
- Plagiarism or unlicensed use of third-party intellectual property
- Publicly disclosing security vulnerabilities without following the responsible disclosure process (see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md))
- Retaliating against anyone who reports a concern in good faith

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All code, issues, pull requests, discussions, and documentation in this repository
- Communications on any platform used by this project (GitHub, Discord, email, etc.)
- Any situation where a person is representing Revobots or this project publicly

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

| Severity | Consequence |
|---|---|
| Minor, first offence | Private written warning with explanation |
| Repeated or moderate | Temporary suspension from contributing |
| Serious or harmful | Permanent ban from the project |
| Legal violation | Referral to appropriate legal authorities |

Enforcement decisions are made by the project maintainer and are final.

---

## Special Considerations for Business Use

Because this repository underpins **real customer deployments**:

- Contributors must never knowingly introduce regressions that could affect production robotic systems without flagging them explicitly in the PR
- Breaking changes to robot interfaces, dataset schemas, or policy APIs require maintainer sign-off before merge
- Any contribution touching safety-critical code paths (motor limits, emergency stop, joint velocity caps) is subject to mandatory review
